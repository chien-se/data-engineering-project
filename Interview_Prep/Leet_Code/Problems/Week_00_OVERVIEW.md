## 🚀 Daily Study Routine (45-60 min optimal)

**Day 1-2 (New Problem Type):** 45 min
- 10 min: Pattern recognition
- 10 min: Read one solved example
- 15 min: Attempt problem independently
- 10 min: Compare with solution

**Day 3-4 (Practice):** 45 min
- 5 min: Review pattern
- 15 min: Solve first practice problem
- 15 min: Solve second practice problem
- 10 min: Analyze approach differences

**Day 5 (Deep Dive):** 45 min
- 20 min: Solve medium practice problem
- 15 min: Optimize solution
- 10 min: Write alternate approach

**Day 6-7 (Week Review):** 30-45 min
- Review all problems solved
- Identify pattern connections
- Note personal edge cases
